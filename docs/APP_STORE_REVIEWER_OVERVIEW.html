AERIS Reviewer Overview

What AERIS is

AERIS is a workplace point-of-sale (POS) client for small retail businesses already running an AERIS workspace. The mobile app is a satellite for sales staff and managers: scan products, build a cart, take payment, look up customers, view past transactions, and process refunds. The five core surfaces are native iOS; an optional ERP tab (hidden by default) wraps the merchant's own admin web UI.

Accounts cannot be created in-app. AERIS workspaces are provisioned on the AERIS platform by the business owner; staff are added by the workspace administrator. The mobile app only signs into an existing workspace.

Test workspace

Sign-in credentials are provided in the App Review Information field. The demo workspace is pre-seeded with sample products, customers, and recent transactions. No real-money payment is taken; the Cash tender is a recorded sale only.

To test the barcode scanner: the demo workspace has no real-world barcode pre-assigned, so before you scan, open any product (Items tab → tap a product), tap Edit, scan or type any barcode you have to hand into the Barcode field (the back of a snack packet, library book, or a generated barcode on screen all work), and tap Save. You can now scan that same barcode from the Items tab search row or from Quick Sale to look the product up.

AERIS How to test

Golden path: full sale + refund

  1. Launch → splash transitions to Login.
  2. Sign in using the credentials provided in App Review Information. You land on the Dashboard with today's sample numbers.
  3. Scan a product: Items tab → tap the barcode icon in the search row → camera opens. Point at any EAN-13 or Code-128 barcode (sample SKU + barcode in the demo workspace notes). On match, the product detail opens.
  4. Build a sale: Sale tab → tap product tiles to add → tap the cart icon → tap Checkout.
  5. Take payment: Choose Cash → enter any amount ≥ total → tap Complete Sale. A receipt screen appears.
  6. View it: Transactions tab → tap the new sale at the top. You'll see items, payments, and customer.
  7. Refund: tap Refund on the sale detail → choose By amount → enter an amount ≤ sale total → enter a reason → confirm. The sale's Refunds card updates inline with a Net Total below.

Test the "Remove from this device" flow (5.1.1(v))

  1. From any screen, tap the gear icon (top right) → scroll to Danger zone.
  2. Tap Remove account from this device. A destructive iOS alert appears.
  3. Tap Remove. The app signs you out and returns to Login. The server account is unaffected.
Why this is device-only: AERIS accounts are administrator-provisioned (see "Account creation" above). Apple Guideline 5.1.1(v) requires in-app account deletion only when accounts can be created in-app, which AERIS does not. The in-app action is deliberately device-scoped: it clears the sign-in, PIN, biometric preference, cached workspace catalogue, and named cashier sessions. Permanent account deletion is administrator-mediated, as stated in the helper text above the button.
